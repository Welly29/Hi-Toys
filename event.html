Title: Event In Jakarta
Date: 5-7 July 2017
Time: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM
Season: Japan Festival
Location: Jakarta Convention Center
Country: Jakarta, Indonesia

Description: Japan Festival is a vibrant celebration of Japanese culture, featuring a variety of activities and performances that showcase the rich traditions of Japan. Visitors can immerse themselves in the world of anime and manga, enjoy traditional music and dance performances, savor delicious Japanese cuisine, participate in workshops on origami and calligraphy, and explore a marketplace filled with authentic Japanese products.

Title: Event In Bogor
Date: 20-21 March 2018
Time: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Season: Indonesia Games Championship
Location: Bogor Botanical Gardens
Country: Bogor, Indonesia

Description: The Indonesia Games Championship is a highly anticipated gaming event that brings together gaming enthusiasts from across the country. It serves as a platform for gamers to showcase their skills, compete in various gaming tournaments, and engage in friendly competitions. The event features a wide range of popular video games, including multiplayer online battle arenas (MOBAs), first-person shooters (FPS), fighting games, and sports simulations.

Title: Event In Surabaya
Date: 12-14 October 2020
Time: 4:00 PM - 11:00 PM
Season: Matsuri Night Market
Location: Surabaya Town Square
Country: Surabaya, Indonesia

Description: Matsuri Night Market brings the lively atmosphere of Japanese night markets to Indonesia. The event features a vibrant blend of food stalls, game booths, and entertainment that showcases the energetic spirit of Japanese festivals. Attendees can savor a wide array of Japanese street food, try their luck at traditional carnival games, watch exhilarating taiko drum performances, and participate in cosplay contests.

Title: Event In Bali
Date: 9-11 September 2021
Time: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Season: Ikebana Exhibition
Location: Hotel Bali
Country: Bali, Indonesia

Description: The Ikebana Exhibition showcases the artistry and elegance of Japanese flower arrangement, known as ikebana. Held at the prestigious Bali Art Center, this exhibition presents breathtaking displays of ikebana creations by renowned Japanese floral artists. Visitors can admire the meticulous craftsmanship and harmonious compositions of these floral arrangements, attend workshops to learn basic ikebana techniques, and appreciate the inherent beauty of nature expressed through the art of ikebana.

Title: Event In Jakarta
Date: 18-22 November 2022
Time: 12:00 AM - 8:00 PM
Season: Japanese Film Festival
Location: Cinemateque XXI, Jakarta
Country: Jakarta, Indonesia

Description: The Japanese Film Festival is a showcase of contemporary Japanese cinema, offering a diverse selection of films from various genres.
